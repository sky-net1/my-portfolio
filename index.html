Case Study

BigTaste Mobile & Web App

Introduction: BigTaste is a food delivery mobile service that aims to simplify the process of ordering food from the restaurant located across Nigeria. As the lead UX designer, I was responsible for designing their mobile app to enhance the user experience and drive customer engagement.

Problem Statement: Users are not satisfied with their delivery time and random cancellations. They also complained about a mobile interface with a clustered interface, a confusing navigation, and lacked visual appeal, thus leading to a poor user experience. Users Suggest an app which can solve these problems and increase user interaction. The goal was to create a user-friendly app that would streamline the ordering process and increase customer satisfaction.

Research and Analysis: To understand user preferences and pain points, I conducted user interviews, surveys, and analyzed competitor apps. Key insights revealed that users valued simplicity, intuitive, quick access to menus, and reliable delivery services.

Design Process:
1. Information Architecture: I developed user flow diagrams to ensure a logical and intuitive navigation structure. This included optimizing the registration process, improving restaurant browsing, and simplifying the menu viewing experience.
2. Wireframing: Low-fidelity wireframes were created to establish the layout, content placement, and hierarchy of each screen. This helped to visualize the app's structure and interactions.
3. Visual Design: With a focus on branding and visual appeal, I developed a modern and vibrant visual identity for BigTaste. This involved creating a new logo, selecting a fresh color palette, and choosing suitable typography and imagery.
4. Prototyping: Using design tools like Figma and Abobe XD, I built interactive prototypes to demonstrate the app's functionality. This allowed for testing and refinement before moving into the development phase.

Testing and Iteration: I conducted usability tests with potential users, gathering feedback on the app's navigation, menu browsing, ordering process, and overall satisfaction. The feedback helped identify pain points and areas for improvement, which were then incorporated into the design.

Results and Impact: Following the redesign, BigTaste experienced a 35% increase in user engagement, a 30% rise in orders placed through the app, and positive feedback from users praising the app's intuitive interface. The improved user experience resulted in higher customer satisfaction and increased brand loyalty.

Conclusion: The BigTaste mobile app redesign successfully addressed usability issues, created an intuitive ordering experience, and improved customer engagement. By applying a user-centered design approach, we were able to enhance the app's functionality and visual appeal, resulting in a positive impact on BigTaste’s business objectives.

StreamlineTravel - Vacation Booking App

Introduction:
StreamlineTravel is a vacation booking app designed to simplify the process of planning and booking vacations for travelers. The app provides users with a user-friendly interface, personalized recommendations, and a seamless booking experience. This case study explores the development and success of StreamlineTravel, highlighting key features, challenges faced, and strategies implemented to enhance user experience and drive growth.

Problem Statement:
1. Complexity and Information Overload: Navigating multiple websites, comparing prices, and finding the best deals for flights, accommodations, and activities can be time-consuming and overwhelming. Users often struggle to gather relevant information and make informed decisions.
2. Lack of Personalization: Many existing vacation booking platforms do not adequately cater to the individual preferences and interests of users. The absence of personalized recommendations often leads to a generic and impersonalized vacation planning experience.
3. Inefficient Search and Filtering Options: Finding specific travel options based on location, price range, amenities, and other criteria can be challenging. Users may encounter difficulties in refining their search results to match their specific requirements.
4. Insecure Booking and Payment Processes: Users may have concerns regarding the security and reliability of the booking and payment systems offered by vacation booking platforms. Trust and confidence in the transaction process are crucial for users to proceed with their bookings.

Design Goals:
The primary objective of StreamlineTravel is to create a one-stop solution that empowers users to plan and book their vacations easily and efficiently. The app aims to streamline the entire vacation booking process, eliminating the need to navigate multiple platforms or engage in extensive research.

Key Features:
1. Intuitive User Interface: StreamlineTravel offers a visually appealing and intuitive user interface, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable user experience. The interface is designed to be accessible on both mobile devices and desktop platforms, providing consistent usability across different devices.
2. Personalized Recommendations: By leveraging machine learning algorithms and user preferences, StreamlineTravel offers personalized vacation recommendations based on factors such as destination preferences, budget constraints, travel dates, and past booking history. This feature enables users to discover tailored options that align with their individual preferences and interests.
3. Comprehensive Search and Filtering Options: The app incorporates robust search and filtering capabilities, enabling users to narrow down their vacation options based on criteria such as location, price range, accommodation type, amenities, and more. This ensures that users can find precisely what they're looking for without spending excessive time and effort.
4. Secure Booking and Payment Process: StreamlineTravel ensures a secure and reliable booking process by partnering with trusted payment gateways and implementing encryption protocols to protect user information. The app accepts multiple payment options, providing flexibility and convenience to users.

Challenges Faced:
1. Integrating Multiple Third-Party APIs: StreamlineTravel faced challenges in integrating with various third-party APIs to fetch real-time availability, pricing, and booking information from airlines, hotels, car rental services, and tour operators. Ensuring smooth and reliable data synchronization required rigorous testing and continuous maintenance.
2. Managing Inventory and Availability: Maintaining accurate and up-to-date information about hotel availability, flight schedules, and other travel services proved challenging due to frequent changes and cancellations. StreamlineTravel implemented automated systems to synchronize data and promptly update users about any changes in their bookings.

Strategies Implemented:
1. Continuous User Feedback and Iteration: StreamlineTravel actively sought user feedback and incorporated it into regular iterations of the app. By listening to user suggestions, identifying pain points, and addressing usability issues, the app improved its functionality and user satisfaction over time.
2. Seamless Integration of Reviews and Ratings: The app integrated customer reviews and ratings for accommodations, airlines, and other travel services. This feature helped users make informed decisions and enhanced the overall credibility of the booking platform.
3. Partnerships and Loyalty Programs: StreamlineTravel formed strategic partnerships with travel service providers to offer exclusive deals, discounts, and loyalty programs for its users. These collaborations helped attract more users to the app and fostered customer loyalty.

Results and Impact:
StreamlineTravel witnessed significant growth and user adoption since its launch. The app's user base expanded steadily, and positive reviews and recommendations contributed to increased brand recognition. Key performance indicators, such as the number of bookings, average user ratings, and revenue, consistently showed upward trends.

Conclusion:
StreamlineTravel's success lies in its ability to simplify the vacation booking process while providing a personalized and seamless experience for its users. By leveraging user feedback, strategic partnerships, and robust technology infrastructure, the app emerged as a trusted platform for travelers worldwide, making vacation planning and booking hassle-free and enjoyable.
